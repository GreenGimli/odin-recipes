Italian Beef Sangwiche

Description

Now this right here is a damn good sangwiche. My gumar used to make dis one for me all da time. Wit da peppers and da juices...oh baby. Remember, ya gotta baptize it!

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds chuck roast
  • 3 (1 ounce) packages dry Italian salad dressing mix
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 (16 ounce) jar pepperoncini peppers
  • 8 hamburger buns, split

Steps

  1. Place the roast into a slow cooker, and season with Italian dressing mix.
  2. Pour in the water.
  3. Cover, and cook on High for 6 to 7 hours.
  4. During the last hour, shred the meat with two forks - if it does not shred easily, cook longer.
  5. Add the peppers, and as much of the juice as you like for additional flavor.
  6. Serve on buns.
