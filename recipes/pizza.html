Pepperoni Pizza

Description

Quick, Easy and Delicious-Homemade pizza crust and tomato sauce has never been easier-and this recipe for pepperoni pizza produces a delicious classic!

Ingredients

  • Rao's Tomato Sauce
  • Pizza crust
  • 1 (6 ounce) package pepperoni
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese, or more to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F.
  2. Top crusts with sauce, pepperoni and cheese.
  3. Bake for 18 to 20 minutes until crusts are browned and cheese is bubbly. For best results, rotate pizza pans between top and bottom oven racks halfway through baking.
